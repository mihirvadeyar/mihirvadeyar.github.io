About me

Hello! I’m Mihir Vadeyar, a passionate Backend Developer with a strong interest in building scalable, reliable, and high-performing software systems. My journey in software development began with a curiosity about how applications work behind the scenes, and that curiosity has evolved into a passion for designing the services and infrastructure that power seamless user experiences.

I enjoy solving complex technical challenges, whether it’s designing robust APIs, optimizing data access patterns, improving application performance, or building resilient distributed systems. My approach is grounded in a strong foundation of Java, databases, API development, and software design principles, with a focus on creating maintainable and scalable solutions that can grow with business needs.

Beyond technical implementation, I am fascinated by system architecture, scalability, reliability, and the engineering practices that help modern applications operate efficiently at scale. I enjoy continuously expanding my knowledge, exploring emerging technologies, and learning new tools and frameworks that enable better software development.

I believe that great engineering is a combination of technical excellence, continuous learning, and collaboration. This mindset has helped me adapt to new challenges, contribute effectively across teams, and consistently seek opportunities to improve both systems and processes.

When I’m not coding, you can find me enjoying sports like soccer and badminton, solving strategy-based board games, or exploring new technologies and ideas within the software engineering world.

I’m always excited to connect with fellow developers, technology enthusiasts, and organizations building impactful products. Thank you for visiting my portfolio, and I look forward to creating something meaningful together.
